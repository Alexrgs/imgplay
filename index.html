imgplay

Image sequence player

Why?

Sometimes you need to show a sequence of images in the same way you play a video. Think of the case that you have a set of time lapse pictures captured using a webcam and you just want to show them on your website. Usinng a general image carousel might not come with an API better enough to control the play or does not support dynamic loading of images. Simply you need a player to play your images or slides. Try this!

Basic setup

HTML

<div id="imageplayer" class="imageplayer">
    <img src="image1.jpg" />
    <img src="image2.jpg" />
    <img src="image3.jpg" />
    <img src="image4.jpg" />
    <img src="image5.jpg" />
    <img data-src="image6.jpg" />
    <img data-src="image7.jpg" />
    <img data-src="image8.jpg" />
    <img data-src="image9.jpg" />
    <img data-src="image10.jpg" />
</div>
JavaScript

$('#imageplayer').imgplay();
